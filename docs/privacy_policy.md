Privacy Policy
Effective Date: January 2025
Process Cards (“the App”) is designed to respect your privacy. All data created in the App remains under your control.
Information We Collect
Process Cards does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.
The App may access the following only when you explicitly choose to use them:
Camera – to attach photos to steps
Photo Library – to select or save images
Files – to export projects as PDF or DOCX
How Your Data Is Used
All projects, steps, images, and exports are stored locally on your device
Data is used only to provide core app functionality
No data is sent to external servers
Data Storage
All content is stored on your device
Images saved to your photo library remain under your control
Deleting a project or step removes associated data from the app
Data Sharing
Process Cards does not share data with third parties
No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used
Internet Usage
The App does not require an internet connection for normal use
No background network communication occurs
Children’s Privacy
Process Cards does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

This policy may be updated to reflect app changes. Updates will be posted at this URL.
